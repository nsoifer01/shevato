Free Web Apps

Six free web apps we have built and keep online. No ads, no signup required, and they all save locally by default with optional Firebase sync if you want to log in.

Mario Kart Tracker

Log Mario Kart races, track player stats over time, and dig into performance with charts and achievements. Supports Mario Kart 8 Deluxe and Mario Kart World.

Track Races

Gym Tracker

An installable PWA for logging workouts, building programs, and watching your numbers move. Works offline once installed.

Start Tracking

Football H2H League

Head-to-head football match recorder. Scores, penalty shootouts, team selections, and a player comparison table for the long-running rivalry at home.

View League

Rising Seasons

Find TV shows by the shape of their episode ratings. Rising, consistent, slow-burn, big-finale, or rebound seasons across thousands of shows.

Browse Seasons

MapTap Rivals

Log daily MapTap.gg head-to-head scores against named friends. Win streaks, averages, per-rival dashboards, time-boxed rivalry seasons, and a calendar heatmap of daily activity.

Track Rivals

Arena

Real-time multiplayer hub for private rooms with friends. Create a room, share an invite link, and play Globe Drop geography, Trivia, and more.

Open Arena
