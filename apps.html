Free Web Apps

Seven free web apps we have built and keep online. No ads, no signup required, and they all save locally by default with optional Firebase sync if you want to log in.

An installable PWA for logging workouts, building programs, and watching your numbers move. Works offline once installed.

Real-time multiplayer hub for private rooms with friends. Create a room, share an invite link, and play across a growing collection of games: Globe Drop geography (pin cities on a 3D globe), head-to-head Trivia, and more on the way.
